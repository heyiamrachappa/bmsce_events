# Club Organizer Payment Setup

Welcome, Organizer!

BMSCE Events now supports connecting your personal or club Razorpay account to directly receive payments for event registrations.

## How to Connect Your Account

1. Navigate to your **Profile** page on the top right.
2. If you are an active organizer, you will see a section titled **"Payment Account Settings"**.
3. Click the **Connect Razorpay** button.
4. An interface will appear prompting you to map your Razorpay account. Provide your Razorpay Merchant ID or linked email for authentication.
5. Once successfully linked, your account status will show as **Active**.
6. You can now post paid events and set an entry fee!

*(Note: Currently, the platform uses a unified test environment. All checkout flows will simulate payment confirmation without deducting real money. Once your Razorpay account is fully integrated into the production environment, funds will be directly routed to your configured merchant ID automatically.)*

## Admin Step Down

If at any point you wish to surrender your responsibilities, you can securely step down:

1. Go to your **Profile** page.
2. Locate the **"Danger Zone"** towards the bottom.
3. Click **"Step Down as Admin"**. 
4. Please note that this is an immediate, irreversible action. If you step down, you will immediately lose your permissions to create events. The club will be without an admin until a new organizer is approved.

## Viewing Event Payments

- From your **Dashboard**, under the **Event Payments** module, you can see real-time payment transactions for all events hosted by your club.
- Use the quick filters to drill down by Event, or by Status (e.g. pending vs completed).
- A powerful unified table shows Participant Name, USN, Event Name, Amount, Status, and Timestamp.
